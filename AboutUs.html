About Us

Sakina, a Commonwealth Scholar...

Sakina, a Commonwealth Scholar and experienced Education Consultant, is dedicated to supporting students through the complex journey of scholarship applications and university admissions. With firsthand experience as a shortlisted candidate for prestigious scholarships like Fulbright and Chevening, along with nominations for TWO Commonwealth Scholarship programs, she brings unmatched expertise and insight.

Holding a Master’s in Education from Liverpool Hope University, UK, and a Bachelor’s in Economics from LUMS, Sakina has worked with the admissions offices of various UK universities. She has guided numerous students through the study abroad and scholarship process, empowering them to navigate the challenges of admissions and secure places at top universities with competitive scholarships.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Continuous Support

Ongoing support and guidance throughout the admission process. Responsive to student needs and regularly monitor progress. Strategic mentorship for those who may not initially achieve desired admissions.

Client-First Approach

Transparent communication channels to keep clients informed and empowered. Special discounts and tailored packages available for students with financial constraints. Proactive support and personalized strategies tailored to each client’s unique situation.

Comprehensive & Tailored Approach

In-depth understanding of individual goals to offer personalized support. Personalized solutions for each student’s unique needs and learning style. Services include admissions guidance, career counselling, and funding assistance. Holistic approach covering guidance on CVs, references, and other tests.

MISSION & VISION

At EdAdvisory, our goal is to empower students and professionals by providing tailored, comprehensive guidance for academic and career success. We are dedicated to bridging the gap between potential and achievement, enabling individuals to access opportunities, secure scholarships, and gain admission to top institutions worldwide.

— Sakina Babar, Founder
